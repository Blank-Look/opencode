# Runbooks Overview

Runbooks are step-by-step operational procedures that enable consistent, repeatable execution of routine and emergency tasks. They serve as the single source of truth for **how** we do things.

## Purpose

- Reduce errors by standardising procedures
- Enable any team member to perform critical tasks
- Speed up incident response with checklists
- Onboard new team members faster

## Runbook Format

Each runbook follows a standard structure:

1. **Description** — what this runbook covers
2. **Prerequisites** — access, tools, permissions needed
3. **Steps** — numbered, detailed instructions
4. **Verification** — how to confirm success
5. **Rollback** — how to undo if something goes wrong
6. **References** — related docs and contacts
