Processes Overview

Standardised processes ensure consistent, auditable, and efficient ICT operations. Our processes are aligned with ITIL 4 best practices, scaled for a small team.

Process Framework

We maintain five core processes, covering the full lifecycle of ICT service management:

Process ITIL Practice Purpose
Change Management Change Enablement Control modifications to ICT systems
Incident Management Incident Management Restore normal service after disruptions
Request Fulfillment Service Request Management Handle standard user requests
Problem Management Problem Management Identify and resolve root causes

Process Ownership

Each process has a designated process owner responsible for:

  • Maintaining process documentation
  • Ensuring compliance with the defined workflow
  • Reviewing and improving the process annually
  • Reporting process metrics to management

Continuous Improvement

All processes are subject to regular review using the Deming cycle (Plan-Do-Check-Act). Improvement suggestions can be submitted via the ticketing system.
