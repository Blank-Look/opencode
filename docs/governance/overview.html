Governance Overview

ICT governance ensures that the team's activities align with organisational objectives, regulatory requirements, and industry best practices. It provides the framework for decision-making, accountability, and performance measurement.

Purpose

Establish clear authority, responsibility, and processes for managing ICT resources, risks, and compliance. Good governance enables the team to deliver value while managing risk effectively.

Scope

This section covers:

  • ICT policies and standards
  • Compliance obligations and audits
  • Risk management framework
  • Vendor and third-party governance
  • Data governance and privacy

Governance Framework

Our governance model is based on COBIT 2019 and ITIL 4 principles, adapted for a small ICT team. Key components include:

Component Description Owner
Policy Framework Documented policies and standards ICT Manager
Compliance Register Regulatory and contractual obligations Compliance Lead
Risk Register Identified risks, controls, and treatments Risk Owner
Review Cycle Quarterly policy reviews and annual audits ICT Manager
