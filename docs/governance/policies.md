# ICT Policies

Policies define the rules and standards that all team members and users must follow. They are approved by management and reviewed annually.

## Policy Hierarchy

```
Policy (high-level rules)
  └── Standard (specific mandatory requirements)
       └── Guideline (recommended practices)
            └── Procedure (step-by-step instructions)
```

## Policy Template

Each policy should include:

1. **Purpose** — why the policy exists
2. **Scope** — who and what it applies to
3. **Policy Statements** — the mandatory rules
4. **Roles and Responsibilities** — who does what
5. **Compliance** — how compliance is measured
6. **Exceptions** — how exceptions are granted
7. **Review Date** — next scheduled review

## Creating a New Policy

1. Draft the policy using the template
2. Consult with relevant stakeholders
3. Submit to ICT Manager for review
4. Legal review if applicable
5. Management approval
6. Publish and communicate to all staff
7. Schedule review date
