Maintenance

Ongoing management and maintenance of ICT assets throughout their operational life.

Maintenance Activities

Activity Frequency Owner
Firmware updates Quarterly Operations Lead
Patch management Weekly Operations Lead
Hardware health check Monthly Team member
Licence compliance audit Quarterly ICT Manager
Warranty review Annually ICT Manager
Performance review Bi-annually Operations Lead

Support and Warranty

  • Log all maintenance activities in the asset register
  • Track warranty expiry dates with 60-day reminder
  • Extended support contracts: renew at least 30 days before expiry
  • For critical assets, maintain 24/7 vendor support

Software Licence Management

Action When
Review licence utilisation Monthly
Reclaim unused licences Quarterly
Renew subscriptions 30 days before expiry
Audit compliance Annually

Refresh Cycle

Asset Type Typical Life Cycle Refresh Trigger
Laptop 3-4 years End of warranty, performance degradation
Server 4-5 years End of vendor support, capacity limits
Network switch 5-7 years End of support, bandwidth requirements
Firewall 3-5 years End of support, security feature gap
Phone 3-4 years Battery degradation, OS support end
